02 / About

A designer's eye, a developer's hands.

My path here was anything but linear. I studied ballet dancing at an art school, graduated in Communication and Public Relations, and eventually chose software development as my main career — because the web finally let me bring all those creative worlds together.

Today I focus on front-end development and web design — translating brand and user needs into clean layouts, considered type, and interfaces that feel as good as they look.

I obsess over micro-interactions, spend too long choosing the perfect easing curve, and genuinely believe great products start with empathy for the user.

I'm also an AI enthusiast. A few months ago I completed an AI Instructional Design course and I'm now a Certified AI-Powered Instructional Design Professional — I love exploring how AI can shape better learning experiences and smarter products.

When I'm offline, you'll find me at the gym, exploring photography and video editing, making art, hiking mountain trails, or moving through ballet and yoga.
